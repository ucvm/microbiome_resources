Microbiome resources

A collection of good journal articles related to methods used to study the microbiome. Unlike the popular Microbiome Digest the purpose of this page is to provide a list of curated foundational papers that, for the most part, will remain static. Contributions, in the form of pull requests on the github page are welcome. Please include a description of the paper indicating its importance to the field.
