Microbiome resources

A collection of good journal articles related to methods used to study the microbiome. Contributions, in the form of pull requests on the github page are welcome. Please include a description of the paper indicating its importance to the field.

Must Reads

Foundational methods paper describing how to do amplicon sequencing data using paired-end sequencing on the MiSeq.

  • Kozich JJ, Westcott SL, Baxter NT, Highlander SK, Schloss PD. Development of a Dual-Index Sequencing Strategy and Curation Pipeline for Analyzing Amplicon Sequence Data on the MiSeq Illumina Sequencing Platform. Appl Environ Microbiol. American Society for Microbiology (ASM); 2013;79: 5112–5120. link

The ‘kitome’ paper. Why blanks are absolutely essential

  • Salter SJ, Cox MJ, Turek EM, Calus ST, Cookson WO, Moffatt MF, et al. Reagent and laboratory contamination can critically impact sequence-based microbiome analyses. BMC Biology 2015 13:1. BioMed Central; 2014;12: 1. link

Why it’s really not a good idea to ‘rarefy’. Also good evidence for using DESeq2 for identification of differentially abundant OTUs

  • McMurdie PJ, Holmes S. Waste Not, Want Not: Why Rarefying Microbiome Data Is Inadmissible. McHardy AC, editor. PLoS Comp Biol. Public Library of Science (PLoS); 2014;10: e1003531. link
